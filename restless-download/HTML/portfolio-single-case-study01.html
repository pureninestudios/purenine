Creating digital identity and brand new website for Green Tea natural products

We placed ourselfs into our clients shoes and tried to create layouts that would be of use for us too, as creative digital agency. We wanted to create something unique, classy, fresh and with style. A layout that drives conversions and helps you grow your business into a larger scale. SOmething that will be memorable, user-friendly and lasting.
